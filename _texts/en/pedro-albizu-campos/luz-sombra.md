For Corretjer: The Homeland is valor and sacrifice. Albizu Campos, Río Piedras, January 20, 1915.

Shadow, dreams, creation — here is the scale of Eternity.
God made everything from Nothing, that is, from the Shadow. From it He also drew the light for human understanding of the creative secret; but the best mystical irradiation is not possible without the light extinguishing the retina and restoring the empire of the Shadow.

Shadow is the soul as a divine and immortal spark. In it is resolved the mystery of light and shadow as in God Almighty.
Twilight, lunar eclipse, solar eclipse, extinction of stars, disappearance of universes, restitution of the mystical shadow, creative matrix, which God will make luminous in his divine power to individualize everything in beings that will always carry with them the perpetual memory of their managing mother, the Shadow.

Bodies dissolve and shadows perpetuate. Immortality is the eternal law.
God enjoys mutations in his own work, and the elements are governed with the internal form, that is, they will grow like his shadow. Morphology is a revelation.

Light as emerged from the arcane, by decree of the Omnipotent, is like the son: it will always project to its own mother, the Shadow.
Initiation is in the understanding of the Shadow. The shadows will speak to us and reveal the great secret; they will sing to us and make audible the harmony of their divine concert.

For comprehension of the All we will extinguish the suns and the stars. We will re-enter the Genitrix Shadow.

By Pedro Albizu Campos
Río Piedras, February 11, 1935.
