To Nationalism, however, they intend to completely dispossess it of all representation in the Insular Electoral Board. Now then, every electoral law is made to give guarantees to the opposition to the government. The governing parties do not need guarantees, because the power does not limit itself to giving them guarantees, but rather grants them all the privileges to keep them strong, because they are the instruments of its policy. Thus the Unión Republicana, the Partido Liberal and the Partido Socialista, while they are as they are, governing parties, the regime will ensure their development by all the means at its disposal, which are all, in a tyrannical foreign intervention like the one that wants to hold our sovereignty.

What is more, to distribute the budget among those parties, -of course, excluding the highly remunerated positions which the Yankee power disposes of at its whim-, elections are not necessary. This observation is so certain that the Yankee power in no way takes into consideration the electoral result when it divides the budget among the governing parties. And it should not be forgotten that all the interference of those parties in the present regime is limited to the participation of their adherents in the subordinate positions of the budget, to handing over, in the sacred name of Puerto Rico, all the vital interests of the Homeland to its interventionist enemies.

The parties called governing parties in this regime, by virtue of the same, are impotent to do anything affirmative in defense of Puerto Rico. If they wanted to take any initiative, they would necessarily run into the interventionist regime, which would deny them the budgetary good and condemn them automatically to extinction. The same insular legislature has served to verify its uselessness to defend the patriotic interests before the absolute veto that gravitates over all its legislation.

We do not want to discourse on the grave responsibility incurred in constituting a "governing" party under foreign military tyranny, such as the one that prevails here. But we must call attention to the fact that there is no right to continue serving as a dupe to the Yankee power to obstruct the development of Nationalism, which represents the militant claim to the absolute independence of the Homeland; to be the sole arbiter of its destinies in all orders of its national life.

We will be told that they want to work stealthily in favor of the patriotic interests in the same enemy tents. That tactic is as ineffective as has been proven in the thirty-five long and terrible years of Yankee and demoralizing intervention, because it tends to weaken the virile fiber of nationality.

We appear through your mediation before the legislative body that you have the honor to preside over, so that the stain that weighs on the history of the legislature for having lent itself to the systematic obstruction of nationalism, which is the only cause that is ours absolutely as Puerto Ricans, is not repeated.

The Partido Nacionalista, despite all the arbitrariness of the regime, has maintained in the polls, in all the elections, from 1920 to here, the protest against foreign interference in the affairs of Puerto Rico, maintaining its right to absolute independence intact. Currently, its ideology informs all public opinion. As the only opposition party, it is the most called upon to have representation in the Insular Electoral Board.

I take the opportunity to express to the Mr. President of the Insular Senate the most distinguished consideration.

S.S.S.,
P. ALBIZU CAMPOS
President of the
Partido Nacionalista

Homage
to Professor Clemente
Pereda
PROCLAMATION

God, who created the clay for the vessel of humanity, deposited in that vessel the happiness of his grace, giving to understand his infinite will that matter will have to adapt to the transcendence of things, in order to establish, under the protection of piety, the equilibrium of love.

It is explained, by the art of affection, that the evocative figures of the supreme have emerged to the light of the world; figures, in their greatest line, that have suffered the obstinacy of the song: poets, liberators, martyrs and saints.

To the category of the poets belong the liberators, the martyrs and the saints, and thus measured in a key of resonance, it is not to be doubted that detachment seizes all of them, who are like wandering kisses of God.

Renunciation seizes them. In what exists a turn of imagination, a lightness, they renounce the barrier of matter and reach with the grace of the spirit. They ascend more the more the pain sinks its goad into the entrails of life and they manage to become ecstatic, redeem themselves, emancipate themselves from the temporal, conquering the bittersweet fruit of the apostolate.

For the souls that overcome the asphyxia of the ephemeral, the apostolate is a goal. In it the effort is recreated, transmuting itself into harmony. The apostle is a perfect music, and hence it is only lawful to compare the spirits of light in a plane of sanctity.

El Møndq, March 29, 1934, p. 6 and 15. Professor Clemente Pereda, professor at the Universidad de Puerto Rico began a seven-day hunger strike in the Pltza Baldorioty in San Juan on March 24, 1934. The purpose of the fast was to protest a joint resolution presented in both chambers requesting that Fuerto Rico be converted into a federated state of Esrados Unidos.

However, matter will not shun the comparison. Inclined to weight and measure, its nature forms number, supplying tangible angles. Matter is almost always a tangent through which the breath of life escapes to occupy its space of infinity.

This does not mean that the spirit of the apostle lacks infinity, while the claw of matter imprisons it; infinite it is and will be, and, consequently, its mission consists in embellishing its vessel of clay, with the splendor of its halo.

In this explicit case, the apostle is obliged by the supreme law to sacrifice himself positively, since the squandering of his beauty will cause the world the pendular movement of death; instead of constituting the touchstone of fecundity, it will be transformed into a negative force, which drags and floods; and what it intends to save with its example, will conclude in disaster, victim of its own spiritual whirlwind. It is imposed, therefore, the foresight, the angelic tact of the anointed, so as not to sacrifice -when being sacrificed for the sake of an idea- what of saintly we protect with the crown of martyrdom. And that protection takes root from the moment that the apostle renounces passivity, doing honor to the artistic sense of his work, because in the positive of the action finds effect the cause of the apostolic virtue. Action, action, there you have the martial step of beauty.

And it is not that there is no action in the penance of a man who fasts. The stoicism of Professor Clemente Pereda acts with mastery when declaring a Holy Week of fasting, when impiety propitiates its bitter food. That man acts individually, while he tastes the pain of all and cleans the sweat of Puerto Rico with the promise fulfilled of his virtue. He acts against the felony of the henchmen, and illuminated with hope, traces for the youth a course of heart. He acts with a Nazarene step and his attitude instills envy in the bronze that was not born with the figure of the liberators.

It is the eloquence of the Homeland. Those who apostrophize it, for the pleasure of rolling yoked to the golden chariot of gluttony, do not know it. But he, who constitutes an admiration for history, couples his matter to the transcendence of things, overcoming himself in the insurmountable. Little does the conduct of evil matter, nor does the baptism of good descend on the skeleton of the world, like a rain of freedom.

Those who corner the body of the homeland, desiring to devour it, will not be able to oppose the impetus of the supreme law. While they, sated with unconsciousness, daub their hands with the glitter of the perquisite, and bless the chains that darken contemporary civilization, the finger of God continues to point out the destiny of the peoples. While they candidly accept ANNEXATION (statehood) repudiating the postulates of International Law, which recognizes in it a collective suicide, and tarnishes public and private law and the name of the Nation, by virtue of which no one is in the right to be a slave and much less in the right to violate anyone, enslaving their body and their soul; while they violate human and divine laws, occupying the detestable position of the highest treason. Puerto Rico will continue its triumphal march towards the glory of its INDEPENDENCE.

To carry out that march of miracle until measuring the faith of the men who suffer in the name of freedom; and understanding their sacrifice, until each man crystallizes in his heart the figure of the apostle. It is not lawful to expect others to sacrifice themselves when we lack the will to overcome the sacrifice with weapons in hand. The time has come in which each Puerto Rican is in the duty to be an apostle of his own moral ignition.

Professor Clemente Pereda began the mass of hunger on March 24 and will rise from his torment on Holy Saturday. We cherish the sweetest hope that he will rise from his calvary gloriously, and for this reason the Puerto Rican Nationalism, depository of the faith of the liberators of America, announces to the Nation that on that day of Resurrection, March 31, -holy in the annals of the Church and in those of the Homeland- Puerto Rico is in the sacred duty to render its homage to Professor Pereda, demonstrating thereby that the grace of God sustains those who savor the crust of hunger, having as emblem the cult of Liberty.

At 6 a.m. on March 31 -the day on which Professor Pereda will gird himself with the glory of having concluded his voluntary penance- all the delegations of the Nation will concentrate in the Plaza de Armas of this capital of San Juan Bautista de Puerto Rico and it is hoped that the act will take on all the majesty of a holy Resurrection.
