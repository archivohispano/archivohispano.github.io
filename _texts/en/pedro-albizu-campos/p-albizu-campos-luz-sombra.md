To Lares, to the hallowed land of the Homeland, which guards the sacred ashes of the founders of the Republic, the nation comes to pay homage and to receive the inspiration of the valor and the sacrifice that the heroes of the Revolution embodied.

The independence of the Homeland and its personality as a free and sovereign nation was written with the noblest blood of all generations.

The National Pilgrimage goes to Lares to rescue the will and the valor that we must emulate to restore the Republic.

September 23rd is the day of the heroes and martyrs of independence. Every Puerto Rican will vibrate before the evocation of the heroic anniversary, will feel free of foreign interventions, and will renew the vow to uproot from the Homeland soil the enemy power that attempts to hold national sovereignty.

Lares is the consecration of the nation to armed revolution, the fixed trajectory of all free peoples.

Present generations must write their heroic page in the history of the Homeland. The spirits of the liberators rise in Lares to offer us freedom and the sword to reconquer it.

Given in San Juan de Puerto Rico, on the nineteenth day of September of 1935, the sixty-eighth year of the proclamation of the Republic.

Nationalist Party of Puerto Rico
The National Board:
P. ALBIZU CAMPOS
President
LUIS F. VELÁZQUEZ
General Secretary
