Moments after the session adjourned until nine o'clock this morning, the director of La Palabra, Mr. Juan Antonio Corretjer, made the following statements to one of our reporters:

"With the keenest attention I was listening to the reading that the federal prosecutors Mr. Ortíz Toro and Mr. Snyder made before the federal court and before the gentlemen of the jury of the declaration of Dr. Albizu Campos before the Grand Jury a little over three months ago. Presented as evidence against Mr. Albizu Campos, that document signifies before all men of honor on earth the most beautiful apology that has been made of Mr. Albizu Campos."

"Accused like Mr. Albizu Campos of the great crime of loving the independence of my Homeland and imprisoned for more than one hundred days, I consider that in the company of such a man it is honorable to walk to prison or to death."

"The generous heart of my Homeland, I believe, feels this way."

"In prison or facing death
the vows of
consecration are renewed"

From the Autograph album of Rosa Emilia Albizu Meneses, daughter of Don Pedro Albizu Campos. Collection of Don Francisco Dávila and Doña María M. O'Neill. Puerto Nuevo, Puerto Rico 2 October 1984.
